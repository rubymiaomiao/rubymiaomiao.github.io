Arduino Parking Radar Alarm System

In the embedded course, I learned about basic electronic circuits and the knowledge of using Arduino control board to complete various interactive applications. My team members and I chose a variety of sensors and devices to design the "ultrasonic reversing alarm".

The "Backing Radar Alarm System" can detect the distance between the rear of the vehicle and obstacles to sound the alarm and turn on the warning lights. The "Temperature Display" can detect the outdoor temperature to obtain the air temperature and warn the driver of abnormal ambient temperature.

Through the practical design of each stage and the integration of the theory and technology I have learned into the application of Arduino, I have truly realized the close relationship between hardware and software programming in embedded development, which has improved my own application programming ability. Reports and work presentations have trained my organizational and presentation skills.
